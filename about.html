About

My name is Jason Grigor-Salas and I have always been fascinated by paleontology and archaeology. I have been lucky enough to travel to Guayabo in Costa Rica; Teotihuacan, Tula, and Queretaro in Mexico; Madrid, Barcelona, and Girona in Spain; the Colosseum and the Vatican in Rome and Pompeii in Italy.
